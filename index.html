AI PING PONG GAME


~Developed by Riddhi Patel~










Instructions

  1. keep your laptop screen straight
  2. move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from your laptop screen
  3. move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. now, press the play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from your laptop
  5. the red paddle is your paddle that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
